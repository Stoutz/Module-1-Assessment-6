Description

The PlayStation 5, PS5, is the ninth-generation home video game console created by Sony. It was announced as the successor to the PlayStation 4 in April 2019, was launched on November 12, 2020, in Australia, Japan, New Zealand, North America, and South Korea, and was released worldwide a week later.

Size and Dimension

The PlayStation 5 has a height of 15.4” (390 mm), depth of 10.2" (260 mm), width of 4.1” (104 mm), and approximate weight of 9.9 lb (4.5 kg).
